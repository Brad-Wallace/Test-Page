EYLEA® (aifbercept solution for injection)
Once every 2 monthsa dosage regimen for treatment of wet age-related macular degeneration (AMD)

  • Administration
  • Press Release

The recommended dose of EYLEA is 2mg (equivilant to 50 microliters solution for injection).

EYLEA treatment is initiated with 1 injection per month (every 4 weeks), for 3 consecutive doses,
followed by 1 injection every 2 months (8 weeks). There is no requirment for monitering between injections.

Long term (after 12 months of treatment), it is recommended that patients continue to be treated with EYLEA every 2 months (8 weeks). However, after the first 12 months of treatment with EYLEA, the treatment interval could be extended to every 3 months (12 weeks) based on visual and anatomic outcomes.

EYLEA may be dosed as frequintly as once per month (4 weeks).

*Following 3 initial monthly treatments.
